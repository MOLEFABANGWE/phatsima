Our Story

Phatsima Tuitions was founded by Victor Mmualefe in 2018. An educational consultant, motivational speaker, professional administrator and S.T.E.M Promoter (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) who has worked as a Trainer and Facilitator in Robotics and Programming training young people across the country in STEM. He comfortably boasts of having a solid education background and has worked with various students at different levels of learning in partnership with different schools and tech organizations.
