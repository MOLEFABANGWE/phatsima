About Us

Welcome to our premier primary and secondary homeschooling center of choice! Founded by a dedicated Motswana entrepreneur, we take pride in being a 100% youth-owned learning institute. Offering both part-time tuition and full-time homeschooling programs, we are fully registered and committed to providing top-notch education. As the main author of the highly recommended 90% revision workbooks, our commitment to excellence is evident. Moreover, our extensive partnerships with various schools and provision of home tuitions further enhance our holistic approach to education. Choose us for a transformative learning experience tailored to meet your child's unique needs.
